BRAND

Syntech

Complete brand communications ecosystem: social media, racing livery, and AI-powered school outreach programme

Client

Syntech Biofuel

Role

Creative Director

Year

2024

Scope

Brand Communications, Social Media, Racing Livery, Helmet Design, School Outreach, 3D Rendering, AI Pipeline

The Context

Syntech Biofuel produces the most sustainable commercial fuel in the UK. They needed a design partner who could handle everything from daily social media to high-profile racing sponsorship materials to an educational outreach programme for primary schools. The work demanded both commercial polish and genuine creative ambition, a rare combination.

The Challenge

Build a comprehensive brand communications system covering social media content, brochure design, and strategic documents. Design the racing team livery for the Syntech Racing BTRC programme, including complete helmet design for driver Ryan Smith. Develop and deliver the Carbon Reduction Champion Pack, a school outreach programme using AI-assisted 3D character generation to engage Year 6 pupils in sustainability education.

The Approach

The social media system was built on a modular grid: consistent enough to be recognizably Syntech, flexible enough to cover product announcements, sustainability messaging, and race day content. The racing livery demanded precision: helmet graphics that read at speed, photograph well from any angle, and carry both Syntech and sponsor brands without visual clutter. The school programme was the most inventive. Students drew superhero characters by hand. A custom AI image processing and 3D rendering pipeline transformed those drawings into professional animated characters, live in the classroom.

The Execution

The design work included: complete social media content system with templates, grid layouts, and content guidelines; Syntech Racing helmet design for BTRC driver Ryan Smith featuring Union Jack elements, neon green Syntech branding, and sponsor integration; race day promotional materials and social media content for the British Truck Racing Championship; Carbon Reduction Champion Pack educational materials for UK primary schools; AI-assisted pipeline converting children's hand-drawn characters into 3D animated superheroes; live classroom demonstrations; brochure design, strategy documents, and corporate communications; and vehicle livery concepts for the Syntech fleet.

Outcome

The school outreach programme achieved 100% teacher and student satisfaction. Children who had never interacted with AI technology saw their own drawings transformed into animated 3D characters in real time. The programme was designed to replicate across multiple schools. The racing livery carried Syntech's brand at competitive motorsport events across the UK. The social media system gave Syntech a consistent, professional voice in a market where most competitors still communicate like industrial suppliers.
