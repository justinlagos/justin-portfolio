Vulta

Complete UI/UX design for a multi-chain cryptocurrency wallet — storing, sending, and receiving digital assets across Tron, Lisk, Solana, and Polygon networks with clarity and confidence.

Tron, Lisk, Solana, Polygon

The Context

Vulta is a digital wallet application for storing, sending, and receiving multiple cryptocurrencies across different blockchain networks. In a market where most crypto wallets overwhelm users with technical complexity, Vulta needed design that made multi-chain asset management feel as simple as checking a bank balance. The challenge was designing for two audiences simultaneously: crypto-native users who expected granular control, and newcomers who needed guided, confident interfaces.

The Objective

Design the complete mobile app experience from onboarding through to daily wallet management. Build an intuitive KYC (Know Your Customer) verification flow. Create transaction interfaces that handle the complexity of multi-network transfers without exposing unnecessary technical detail. Ensure the design communicated security and trust — critical for an app that holds real financial assets.

The Approach

The interface used a dark theme — standard in crypto applications, but executed here with restraint and sophistication rather than the neon-heavy aesthetic common in the space. The onboarding flow was designed as a progressive disclosure: create account, set security passcode, verify identity — each step feeling like a natural progression rather than a compliance burden. The wallet dashboard showed portfolio balance prominently with a clear visual hierarchy: total value first, then individual token balances with network indicators. Transaction details were designed to be comprehensive but scannable.

The Execution

Justin designed: the complete onboarding flow — from welcome screen through account creation, security setup, and passcode confirmation; the KYC verification experience with progressive status indicators (pending, reviewing, approved); the portfolio dashboard with real-time balance display, wallet search, and token-specific views; the transaction history with detailed receipt views showing network, memo, and reference data; the send and receive flows with network selection and address management; and the visual system — dark theme with purple accent colours, clean iconography, and clear status communication through colour coding (green for success, amber for pending, red for issues).

The Impact

Vulta's design makes multi-chain cryptocurrency management accessible without dumbing it down. The progressive KYC flow reduces drop-off during a process that typically loses significant users. The clean dashboard design gives users immediate confidence in their portfolio status, while the detailed transaction views provide the transparency that crypto users demand. The design positions Vulta as a serious, trustworthy wallet in a market where too many competitors prioritise visual novelty over functional clarity.
