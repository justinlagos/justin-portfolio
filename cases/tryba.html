PRODUCT

Tryba

End-to-end design for a digital banking platform: Visa card systems, mobile app interfaces, and social media campaigns

Client

Tryba

Role

Product Designer

Year

2022

Scope

Card Design, Mobile App UI, Social Media, Brand Communications, mPOS Terminal

The Context

Tryba is a digital banking platform offering banking, business tools, and payment solutions to individuals and SMEs in Nigeria. In a market crowded with fintech startups all claiming to be intelligent banks, Tryba needed design that was both functionally rigorous and emotionally distinctive: a platform that looked as trustworthy as a traditional bank but felt as intuitive as the best consumer apps.

The Challenge

Design the complete visual identity for Tryba's card products: both personal and business Visa cards. Create the mobile app UI for banking, payments, and mPOS functionality. Develop a social media campaign system that could sustain daily content across multiple platforms while maintaining brand coherence.

The Approach

The card design was treated as a physical brand touchpoint: something users would carry in their wallets and show to others. The business card used a premium dark aesthetic with gold accents; the personal card used Tryba's signature blue with flowing gradients suggesting movement and progress. The app interface prioritized clarity: large readable balances, simple navigation, and a card-management flow that made complex banking operations feel effortless. Social media design used lifestyle photography with bold typography overlays, making banking feel aspirational rather than merely functional.

The Execution

The design work included: Tryba Business Visa card with premium black and gold treatment; Tryba Personal Visa card with signature blue gradients; mobile app interface including dashboard, transaction history, and card management; mPOS interface allowing merchants to accept in-person payments via QR code; social media campaign templates and content across multiple formats; brand communications including app store screenshots and launch materials; and card welcome pack with activation instructions and brand storytelling.

2 Card Products Designed
Full App UI Design
mPOS Payment Interface
Multi Platform Campaigns

Outcome

Tryba's card designs became one of the platform's most recognizable brand assets: the physical embodiment of the digital experience. The social media system enabled consistent daily output without requiring constant creative direction. The mPOS interface opened a new revenue stream by allowing Tryba to serve merchants directly, not just consumers. The design work positioned Tryba as a serious player in Nigeria's competitive digital banking landscape.
