BRAND

Sparkle

Social media design for Nigerian fintech, creating a culturally grounded brand voice

Client

Sparkle

Role

Designer

Year

2022

Scope

Social Media Design, Campaign Visuals, Brand Communications

The Context

Sparkle is a lifestyle and financial ecosystem for Nigeria's retailers, SMEs, and individuals. In a market where every fintech competed with clean, corporate aesthetics, Sparkle wanted to stand out by being genuinely vibrant, culturally grounded, and unapologetically African. The social media presence needed to feel like a lifestyle brand that happened to offer financial services, not a bank trying to be cool.

The Objective

Design a social media content system that could sustain high-volume daily output across Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook while maintaining visual impact and brand coherence. Each post needed to stop a scroll. Campaign themes ranged from product launches and feature announcements to community celebrations and financial literacy content.

The Approach

The design language drew from Afro-pop culture with hand-drawn typography, collage-style photography, and a colour palette built around Sparkle's signature green and yellow. Photography featured real people in real contexts, not stock imagery. Typography was treated as illustration, large and expressive, integrated with photography rather than floating above it. The result was content that felt handmade and personal in an industry dominated by clean corporate templates.

The Execution

The complete social media visual system across multiple platforms; campaign visuals for 'The World Needs to Sparkle' brand campaign; product feature announcements using illustrative typography; the 'Every Small Business Has a Dream' campaign for Sparkle Business launch; community and cultural content celebrating Nigerian holidays and events; and financial literacy content designed to educate without patronising.

The Impact

Sparkle's social media became one of the most distinctive voices in Nigerian fintech, recognisable before you even saw the logo. The cultural grounding of the design work helped Sparkle connect with an audience tired of generic corporate fintech messaging. The 'Every Small Business Has a Dream' campaign generated significant engagement and established a visual language that competitors later attempted to emulate.
