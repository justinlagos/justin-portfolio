Take Back The Mic

Designed the brand, digital environments, and interactive platform behind Africa's largest music and cultural competition — reaching 1.1 billion impressions across 50 countries.

Client

TBTM / In5 Media, Dubai

Role

Brand Design Lead & Digital Creative Director

Timeline

2021–2024 (3 Seasons)

Scope

Brand Identity, Digital Product, Interactive Festival, Card Design, Social Media

Recognition

Webby Award Nomination 2023

The Opportunity

Take Back The Mic is a pan-African music and cultural competition operating across 50 countries, backed by MTN and partnered with Mastercard. It needed a design system that could unify wildly different markets — from Lagos to Nairobi to Dubai — under one visual language, while powering a complex digital product ecosystem including a virtual festival, a crypto rewards system (Kola), and a Mastercard-partnered digital card.

What Was Asked

Build a complete visual identity that works at billboard scale and at app-icon scale. Design an interactive virtual festival platform. Create the UX and visual design for a digital card product. Unify three seasons of content, competition mechanics, and cultural storytelling into a cohesive brand experience that could be deployed simultaneously across dozens of countries.

The Thinking

The brand system was built modular — a core identity kit that could flex across regions without losing coherence. Bold typography, high-contrast colour, and a visual language rooted in energy and cultural pride. The Kola crypto rewards system integrated blockchain mechanics into a familiar entertainment interface — exchange crypto points for mobile data, powered by MTN. The virtual festival was designed as an immersive digital environment, not just a streaming page.

The Work

Across three seasons, Justin designed: the complete brand identity system including logo, typography, colour palette, and brand guidelines; the interactive virtual festival platform (Webby-nominated); the Kola crypto rewards interface and campaign materials in partnership with MTN; the digital card product design that contributed to securing the Mastercard partnership; all social media campaign assets deployed across 50+ countries; outdoor advertising, billboard designs, and environmental graphics; and the complete UX flow for audience participation, voting, and rewards.

The Impact

The design work directly contributed to measurable outcomes at an extraordinary scale. 1.1 billion media impressions across 50 countries. A Webby Award nomination in 2023 for the interactive festival — the internet's most respected international digital award. A multi-year Mastercard partnership secured in part through the quality and ambition of the digital card product design. The brand became synonymous with pan-African cultural ambition — a visual system that worked as well on a Lagos billboard as it did in a Dubai boardroom.
