BRAND

Polo Luxury

Social and email design for West Africa's premier luxury retail destination

Client

Polo Avenue / Polo Luxury

Role

Designer

Year

2022

Scope

Social Media Design, Email Marketing, Campaign Visuals, Product Direction

The Context

Polo Avenue is West Africa's leading luxury retail destination, representing brands including Gucci, Omega, Swarovski, and Cartier. The social media and email design needed to match the expectations of an audience accustomed to international luxury standards while maintaining Polo's distinct Nigerian identity. Every piece of content sat alongside original brand marketing from houses like Omega and Swarovski, so the work had to hold its own in that company.

The Objective

Design social media campaigns and email marketing materials that met international luxury visual standards. Create seasonal campaigns for Easter, Black Friday, and product launches. Develop dedicated content for individual brand partnerships, particularly Omega and Swarovski. Build a visual system that could showcase products with reverence while injecting the personality and cultural relevance that distinguished Polo from generic luxury retail.

The Approach

The design language was deliberately restrained: dark backgrounds, generous white space, and typography that whispered rather than shouted. Product photography was treated as the hero of every composition, with design elements serving as frames rather than distractions. Colour was used sparingly with Polo's signature gold for brand moments and brand-specific palettes for partnership content. The Omega campaign drew directly from the James Bond partnership, creating a visual narrative that felt premium and culturally resonant.

The Execution

Seasonal social media campaigns for Easter, Black Friday, and holiday periods; Omega Seamaster 007 Edition dedicated campaign with product storytelling; Swarovski partnership content with jewellery-focused art direction; email marketing templates for product launches, promotions, and editorial content; the 'Stay Home, Stay Fabulous' campaign responding to COVID-19 lockdowns; and content creation guidelines ensuring visual consistency across a rapidly growing content calendar.

Luxury Brand Standard
Multi Brand Partnerships
2yr Design Tenure
Global Brand Alignment

The Impact

Polo's social media evolved from product-focused posts to a curated luxury editorial experience. The Omega campaign demonstrated that a Nigerian retail brand could create marketing content that sat comfortably alongside the Swiss watchmaker's own international campaigns. The email marketing system drove direct revenue through product-specific storytelling that gave subscribers a reason to open every message.
