PRODUCT

Kavlr

UX/UI design lead for a multi-currency fintech platform with 60+ design pages across a distributed UK and Nigeria team

Client

Kavlr

Role

Lead Product Designer

Year

2023

Scope

Product Design, UX Research, Design System, Landing Pages, Mobile App, Dashboard

The Context

Kavlr is an ambitious fintech platform designed to serve freelancers, small businesses, and creators with multi-currency payments, booking management, invoicing, a marketplace, and social commerce tools. The challenge was not just designing screens but creating a coherent product experience across an unusually wide feature set for users in very different markets, with a team split between the UK and Nigeria.

The Challenge

Design the complete product from landing pages through admin dashboards. Build a scalable design system that could accommodate rapid feature development. Create mobile-first experiences alongside desktop admin tools. Ensure visual consistency across 60+ unique pages while allowing each feature area to feel purposeful and distinct.

The Approach

The design system was built component-first: a library of reusable elements including buttons, form fields, cards, navigation patterns, and modals that could be assembled into any page configuration without losing brand consistency. Color coding was used to differentiate product areas. Bookings, payments, marketplace, and social commerce each had visual cues that oriented users without explicit labeling. The landing page was designed to communicate platform breadth without overwhelming new users.

The Execution

The design work included: complete landing page and marketing website; personal dashboard with transaction history, wallet management, and analytics; business dashboard with invoicing, booking management, and team tools; booking flow covering service selection, scheduling, payment, and confirmation; marketplace interface for discovering and purchasing services; mobile app screens optimized for on-the-go use; Kavlr Embed widget allowing businesses to add booking functionality to external websites; no-code checkout system; and comprehensive design system powering all components.

60+ Design Pages
UK/NG Distributed Team
Full Product Ownership
1 Unified Design System

Outcome

Kavlr's design system enabled the engineering team to build rapidly without sacrificing visual quality. The 60+ page design package gave investors and stakeholders a tangible, polished vision of the product's potential. The cross-country collaboration model became a template for how the company approached distributed product development.
