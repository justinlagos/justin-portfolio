iOS & Android

The Context

HashIT is a fintech platform that merges social engagement with financial rewards — users earn by participating in challenges, voting, and community activities. The platform needed to feel like a social app but function like a financial product, handling real money, KYC compliance, and transaction security while maintaining an interface that users would open daily for entertainment, not just utility.

The Objective

Design the complete product from first principles. Build the information architecture, all user flows, every interface screen, and the visual identity. Create app store marketing assets. Deliver a product that investors could fund, engineers could build, and users would adopt — all in a compressed timeline.

The Approach

The information architecture was designed around two modes: social (discovery, challenges, voting) and financial (wallet, transactions, withdrawal). Navigation made switching between modes seamless. The visual identity used a dark theme with accent colours to create a premium, app-native feel that distinguished HashIT from the bright, busy interfaces of competing Nigerian fintech apps. The onboarding flow was designed to get users to their first social interaction within 60 seconds of opening the app.

The Execution

Justin designed: the complete information architecture and navigation system; all onboarding and authentication flows; the social feed with challenge participation and voting mechanics; the financial dashboard with wallet management and transaction history; the withdrawal and payment flows with security verification; the visual identity system including logo, typography, colour palette, and iconography; app store screenshots and marketing materials; and the crowdpool feature — a community funding mechanism built into the platform.

The Impact

The platform generated NGN 45.5 million in total inflow during the design period — revenue that validated both the product concept and the design decisions. The compressed timeline proved that a single design lead could own an entire product's visual and interaction design without sacrificing quality. The crowdpool feature became one of the platform's most distinctive offerings, differentiating HashIT from standard social apps and standard fintech products alike.
